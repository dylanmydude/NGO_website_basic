About us

Garden Gatherers is an NGO that was established in 2015 with the intention to assist struggling communities with basic needs and to build resilience against economic struggles. Many communities in South Africa are based in semi agricultural areas but they are unable to use the land that is nearest to them.


Garden Gatherers mission is to assist these communities to have a more circular economy, one that is resilient and that also includes:

Food security
Potable water
Waste treatment
Greening the community
Youth development programme
